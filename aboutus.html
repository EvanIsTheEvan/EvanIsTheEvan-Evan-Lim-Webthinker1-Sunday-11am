History

The Byte Busters Coding Club came to life 66 million years ago when a bunch of dinosaurs realized that school was not feeding our appetite for food. Since then, we've grown into a tight-knit community that eats, sleeps, and eats all things.

Our Mission

To fuel our passion for T-rexs and T-rex creation, help each other grow as dinos, and most importantly, to have a ton of fun while doing it.
